Our team is composed of seven students from San Francisco High School’s 12 STEM Watson 2024-2025.

We started EcoBreeze for our Entrepeneurship class performance task, by our teacher Mr. Jarod Ivan W. Palla.

We looked for a problem that our school is currently facing, and we found two.

First, we saw that many students aren’t comfortable in their classrooms because of intense heat.

Second, we saw cluttered plastic bottles around the campus.

We thought of a product that can fix these two problems, and that’s how we came up with EcoBreeze.

Support our mission to create a greener tomorrow for our school, together.
